About us

20+ years of banking & technology, in one team.

RPA at the core

Our major focus is Robotic Process Automation in banking operations — going beyond rule-based processes to deliver smarter, standardized workflows that cut cost and lift collaboration across departments.

Brainotix Solutions was founded with a mission to provide cutting-edge solutions in the banking and financial domain. We are a team of experts with more than 20 years of experience in banking and technology, set on delivering customer-centric, agile, secure and cost-effective solutions to financial enterprises.

With deep experience designing, developing and implementing complex banking solutions — from Core Banking to Digital Banking — we bring a genuine understanding of the banking domain, technology, user behaviour and the future trends shaping the industry, setting new standards in software solution development across multiple platforms.

That understanding now powers a focus on Robotic Process Automation: helping enterprises achieve early cost benefits while optimizing and standardizing processes across departments to drive collaboration and coordination.

Solutions

A scalable, end-to-end enterprise suite.

Twenty-plus years of hands-on delivery across Core Banking, Internet & Mobile Banking, payments, MIS and CRM — combined with best-of-breed tools and industry best practices — in one customer-centric suite.

Procure-to-Pay

An enterprise P2P platform that unifies procurement with accounts payable — streamlining spend, improving supplier evaluation and budget control. One unified suite from purchasing to supplier management, built specifically for banking and financial organizations and aligned to your budgets, discretion and workflows.

Digital Banking

A Digital Banking Suite that helps institutions deliver compelling experiences designed to attract, engage and extend customer relationships. It drives innovation and growth with seamless CX and operational excellence — the foundation for becoming a truly digital, ecosystem-centric bank.

Trade Finance

A solid, secure, multi-currency platform that unifies traditional and contemporary trade finance in one modern architecture. Fully digitalized, extensively parameterized modules cover every aspect of trade transactions, with reusable business components that accelerate innovation-led growth.

Expense Management

An enterprise payment suite that modernizes centralized payment landscapes with proven scalability and agility for changing regulations. Integrates with Core Banking, ERP, HRMS, NSDL, GST Portal, AD, Email & SMS to streamline the full expense cycle — payment to reconciliation — with unified reporting and built-in approval workflows.

Vendor Management

A centralized record of all vendors — their key information, documentation and spend — in one place. Seamless onboarding powered by all-in-one vendor & contract lifecycle management, strict regulatory compliance control, and KPI-based vendor rating for transparent, strategic vendor management.

Vendor Self-Service

A supplier portal that cuts the cost and overhead of invoice capture with a self-service console for vendors. It strengthens buyer–supplier interaction with real-time transaction tracking, straight-through invoice tagging and automated alerts — eliminating repetitive back-and-forth queries.

Fixed Asset Management

Financial tracking across the full asset lifecycle, with barcoding for real-time asset data, repairs and maintenance. It couples with procurement to auto-onboard delivered assets, tags assets to branches and employees, and auto-calculates depreciation — eliminating manual asset reconciliation.

IT Asset Management

Discover, manage and track every IT asset — Windows, non-Windows, VMs and network devices — through simple scanning. A single pane of glass for hardware specs, installed software, scan and ownership history, plus monitoring of switches, routers, gateways and more.

Workflow Management

Easy-to-use automation that standardizes and accelerates your core processes, including budget- and discretion-based approvals. It auto-generates and signs in-flow documents and connects distributed teams across channels — boosting productivity for remote and cross-functional collaboration.

Patch Management System

Centralized, automated patch and vulnerability management across endpoints and servers. It discovers missing patches, schedules policy-driven, staged rollouts, and keeps every device continuously compliant — shrinking your security exposure without disrupting day-to-day operations.

Application Whitelisting

Lock down endpoints so only trusted, approved applications can run. It blocks unauthorized, unknown and malicious software by default and enforces least-privilege execution — hardening your organization against ransomware and zero-day threats across every device.

GST / TDS Reconciliation

Automated reconciliation of GST and TDS data against returns, invoices and the GST portal. It matches input tax credit, flags mismatches and discrepancies early, and generates audit-ready reports — removing manual effort and ensuring accurate, timely statutory compliance.
